Movie Watchlist

bookmark your shows

The Movie Watchlist is a web application that allows users to keep track of their favorite movies, TV shows, and more in one convenient location. Developed using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, this project leverages the Open Movie Database (OMDb) API to fetch movie data based on user inputs.

The Movie Watchlist offers a convenient and efficient way for users to keep track of their favorite movies and shows. Whether you're a movie buff or just looking for something new to watch, the Movie Watchlist has you covered. With its powerful search functionality and easy-to-use interface, the Movie Watchlist is the perfect tool for anyone who loves movies or TV.
